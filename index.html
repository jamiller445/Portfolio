About Me

The Jeffrey Miller story. A native Clevelander - born, educated, and worked for 38 years developing a professional career in Information Technology. I have held various roles including Computer Systems Management, Systems Analyst, and manager of Technology Demonstration Center at NASA Glenn Research Center. I am a husband married for 36 years, farther of two sons and a daughter. A grandfarther of one granddaughter.

I would describe myself as a person with individuallistic style. Motivated to continual learning by the rapid pace of changing technology. I am project oriented and enjoy participating in group work as a contributing team member.

I see my future as being involved in the visual representation and presentation of data. I would also like to augment my current systems knowledge and experience with the ability to develop interactive applications accessible through the internet.
